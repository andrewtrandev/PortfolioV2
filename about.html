About me

I was previously a data entry processor at a fruit/veg wholesaler where I was in charge of processing sales and generating stock reports. I had made scripts to help speed up my work and after some time at the job, I thought about trying to learn more about coding and a career change. I decided to go all in and quit my job, and for the last 5 months I've been doing a coding bootcamp and it's been a challenging but rewarding journey. My strengths are high attention to detail and having a strong drive to continue learning. What I'm looking for is a place that will constantly challenge me and push me to grow.
